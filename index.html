About Us!

Shree shyama chatravas has been conceptualized as a Paying Guest (hostel) in Delhi only for girls, keeping in view the needs of students and professionals for homely stay away from home. Your second home is located in prime location near most popular market in Rohini. Modern construction with all modern and luxurious facilities available. It is one of the best and cost efficient PG for students in Delhi Technological University (DTU), Aditi Mahavidhyala, Shaheed Sukhdev College of Business studies, Bhagvan Parshuram Institute of Technology (BPIT) and more. Also, professionals in Delhi, especially Rohini will find it best suited. It is very spacious, neat & clean and very well maintained which is essential for healthy living. Easy transportation facility through buses, metro, rickshaws and sharing three wheelers at door step. Food served by PG is of best quality. It is quiet evident from the fact that PG owner have the same food served to our guests. Equipped with all the modern aminities, the graceful ambience of rooms promises a soothing and serene stay to our guests. Very safe and secured with multiple biometric lock systems, CCTV surveillance and 24 hour guard. 2 min. walking distance from DTU (250 m)
